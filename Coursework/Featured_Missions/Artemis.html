Artemis Missions

The Artemis missions are a series of planned missions launched by NASA with the intention of returning to the Moon.
The missions will result in long-term presence on and around the Moon.
The Artemis missions will set the stage for further human exploration and future missions to Mars.
The Artemis missions intend to build sustainable infrastructure around and on the surface of the moon to better enable long term exploration.
The Gateway spacecraft, for example, will serve as orbital habitation, a science laboratory, communication hub, and generally an international collaborative project.
It will be the first space station to operate outside of low Earth orbit in human history.
Artemis I, an uncrewed flight test has already sucessfully completed a mission.
Artemis II is due to be the first crewed flight test of the rocket and Orion spacecraft.
After that, Artemis III will facilitate the first crewed landing on the Moon since Apollo 17 in 1972.
